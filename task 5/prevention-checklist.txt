Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always check the website URL before entering details
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Never share OTP with anyone
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Always verify emails and messages before responding
- Regularly update your apps and devices
- Use official apps for UPI payments

Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
