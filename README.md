📌 Project Overview 

This project focuses on understanding digital literacy and its importance in today’s world. It includes creating an infographic, building a digital portfolio, exploring online platforms, writing professional emails, and learning about cybercrime awareness. 

 

 
🟣 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic 

In this task, I created an infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. The infographic includes topics such as meaning of digital literacy, its importance, safe internet practices, and useful digital tools. This task helped me present information in a visual and creative way. 

 

 
 🟣 Task 2: Digital Portfolio 

In this task, I created my digital portfolio using GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a professional online presence. GitHub is used for sharing projects, LinkedIn for networking, and Kaggle for learning data-related skills. This will help me in my future career. 

 

 
🟣 Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms 

In this task, I explored coding and collaboration tools. I solved a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank. I also created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy and generated responses using Google Sheets. This task helped me understand online tools for learning and data collection.

 🟣 Task 4: Email & Digital Etiquette 

In this task, I wrote two professional emails — one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying for an internship. I also created a checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media usage. This task improved my professional communication skills. 

 

 

 🟣 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness 

In this task, I studied phishing as a type of cybercrime. I created a case study explaining how phishing works and its impact. I also made a prevention checklist with safety tips such as not sharing OTPs and avoiding suspicious links. This task helped me understand online safety.
